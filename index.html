About me

I'm a senior operations and customer experience leader with over two decades of experience making things work — really work — for companies and the customers they serve.

From building call centers in Florida while managing teams in Asia, to driving CSAT improvements at ABAX and Fair Car Insurance, I've learned that the gap between strategy and execution is where most companies quietly fail. I live in that gap and help close it.

I'm trilingual, Lean Six Sigma certified, INSEAD-trained, and increasingly obsessed with the intersection of AI and operational excellence. Not in a hype-cycle way — in a "I've tested 20+ tools and here's what actually works" way.

Outside of work, my kitchen is rarely quiet. I cook for a house that's almost always full — family, friends, whoever shows up. My husband is my best travel partner and, conveniently, also my most enthusiastic kitchen helper. It's the thing I find most satisfying: good ingredients, a bit of effort, and people around a table. Our two basset hounds are permanent fixtures at floor level, waiting patiently for something to fall.

"Why should I say anything — no one is going to see this anyway." That customer comment changed the way I lead. Every voice matters. Every signal is data.
